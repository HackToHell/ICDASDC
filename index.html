About the College

K.L.N College fo Information Technology is the first Engineering College of Information Technology in Madurai, started in 2001 by the munificence of philanthropists and well-wishers in Sourashtra Community, which is a linguistic minority in Tamilnadu. The College, a co-educational institution, is sponsored by the K.L.N Sourashtra College of Engineering Council consisting of eminent industrialists and academicians led by the pioneer Amarar Thiru K.L.N.Krishnan.

It is approved by the AICTE, New Delhi and has been affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. The institute is 9001:2008 certified for its quality educational management. The institute offer 5 UG programs and 4 PG programs. The campus is Wi-Fi enabled to cater the needs of the student and staff. The college has signed MoUs with MNC’s like Infosys, IBM, EMC, Oracle, Association of Consulting Civil Engineers, SAP, BSNL, Microsoft, CII and MADITTISSIA.

The Institution is associated with various professional bodies like IEEE, IETE, ISTE, CSI and IEI. AICTE has funded projects and consultancy works are being carried out. Entrepreneurship Development Cell is functioning to work with the Industry Institute Interactions.

About Madurai

Madurai is the second largest city in Tamilnadu located on the banks of the river Vaigai.It has been a major settlement for two milliennia and is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world.Madurai is closely associated with the Tamil language as all three primary congregations of Tamil scholars,the third Tamil Sangams were held in the city.

The city has a number of historical monuments with the Meenakshi Amman Temple and Thirumalai Nayak Palace being most prominent.Madurai is an important and educational hub in south Tamilnadu.

Thiruparankundram is ahill temple which is 8km away form Madurai,where the Hindu god Murugan is said to have married Deivanai.The temple is the first among the six holy abodes of Murugan,the Aarupadai veedu,and one of the most visited tourist spots in Madurai.Alagar Kovil is a temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu and it is situated 21kms away from Madurai city.
